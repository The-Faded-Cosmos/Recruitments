Tefaco

A watch, but not just a watch. A solution. To problems, women's problems, planet's problems. Reality? Not yet. That's why we need you. What we envision is a device capable of neutralizing any threats on a lady whilst also being something a lady would wear. Sounds easy? It ain't. Currently we intend it to have a stun gun built in. But we are ready to see where you take this.
